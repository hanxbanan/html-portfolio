The Best Movies According to Hanna

Hanna's Top 3 Movies of All Time

Twilight

Cool vampire movie based on a book series I read. Features vegetarian vampires that feed on animal blood and sparkle in the sun.

Inception

Trippy movie starring one of my fave actors, Leonardo di Caprio. Dream within a dream. So many memes from this movie.

The Hunger Games: Ballad of Songbirds & Snakes

Another movie based on a dystopian world called "Panem" where one male and one female from each of the 12 Districts is selected to compete in "The Hunger Games" pioneered by a young Coriolanus Snow.
